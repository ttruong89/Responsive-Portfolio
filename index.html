About Me

I was born in Vietnam and raised in Houston, TX. The transition to the English language and culture was tough at first, but over time, it got easier.

Some of my favorite hobbies are working out, basketball, and just hanging out with friends during my free time. I also trade stocks and forex when I am bored. I also enjoy cooking a lot, and love cooking for others.

My current job title is senior seismic analyst for one of Houston's largest seismic data libraries. I hope to pick up web design as another hobby and possibly build a career from it.
